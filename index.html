Jake the Dog!

Jake the Dog is a character and one of the two protagonists in the American animated television series 'Adventure Time' created by Pendleton Ward. He is voiced by John DiMaggio. Jake is accordingly 28 years old in 'magical dog years.' Jake can stretch, shrink, or mold any part of his body to any shape and almost any size, ranging from becoming gigantic to becoming incredibly small. He also acts as a confident mentor to his energetic adopted brother Finn the Human.

"To live life, you need problems. If you get everything you want the minute you want it, then whats the point of living?"
